Physics computes.
We make it run AI.

To sort a bundle of spaghetti by length, drop them on a table — the shortest fall out first. O(1). Gaudí calculated the Sagrada Família's arches not with equations but with hanging ropes, letting gravity find the optimum. The brain runs on 20 watts.

Unconventional computing exploits exactly this — physical processes as primitive operations. We build the software layer that maps AI models onto that substrate.

What we do

Model transformation

Any existing model — transformer, diffusion, graph network — has a unique way of being retrained or restructured for inference on unconventional silicon. We find it and automate it.

Compiler & runtime

A hardware-agnostic intermediate representation with backends for spiking, analog, stochastic, and thermodynamic accelerators. One toolchain, many chips.

Hardware partners

We target processors from the frontier of unconventional compute: Innatera (Netherlands) — neuromorphic spiking processors; Extropic AI and Normal Computing — thermodynamic chips; Unconventional AI — and others pushing the boundary of what silicon can be. Long-term, we vertically integrate by designing our own.
