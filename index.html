Physics computes.
We make it run AI.

To sort spaghetti by length, drop the bundle on a table — the shortest fall out first. O(1). Gaudí designed the Sagrada Família's arches not with equations but with hanging ropes, letting gravity find the optimum. The brain runs on 20 watts.

Unconventional computing exploits exactly this — physical processes as primitive operations. The result: inference in 2–5 ns and energy costs divided by 1000. We build the software layer that gets your model there.

What we do

Model transformation

Any existing model — transformer, diffusion, graph network — has a unique way of being retrained or restructured for inference on unconventional silicon. We find it and automate it.

Compiler & runtime

A hardware-agnostic compiler that translates your model into the native language of each chip — spike trains, stochastic bits, thermodynamic fluctuations. Bring your model; we handle the rest.

Hardware partners

We target the frontier of unconventional compute: Innatera (Netherlands) — neuromorphic spiking processors; Extropic AI and Normal Computing — thermodynamic chips; Unconventional AI — and others pushing the boundary of what silicon can be. Long-term, we design our own.
